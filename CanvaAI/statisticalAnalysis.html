üî¨ Interpretation Guide

Small Error Bars ‚Äî High Precision

‚úÖ Indicates: Good experimental technique, controlled conditions

Large Error Bars ‚Äî High Variability

‚ö†Ô∏è Action: Increase sample size (n) or review technique

Non-Overlapping Bars

üìù You can confidently claim a difference exists

üìù More trials or statistical tests needed to confirm
